# Unhireable Roadmap Snapshot

## Phase 1 — Personal Job Co-Pilot (Now → 3 Months)
- Harden Chrome/Playwright automation with ATS-specific flows, smart waits, and retry logic.
- Enforce profile validation and document generation (resume, cover letter, outreach templates).
- Deliver end-to-end job discovery, application tracking, and activity logging inside the desktop app.

## Phase 2 — Pro Automation Suite (3 → 9 Months)
- Add API integrations with top ATS (Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, SmartRecruiters, Zoho).
- Introduce browser pooling, selector learning, and batch apply with live progress visibility.
- Layer in analytics dashboards (success rate, time per application, ATS coverage) and notification workflows.

## Phase 3 — Two-Sided Platform (9 → 18 Months)
- Launch employer portal: unified job posting, candidate inbox, pipeline management.
- Ship AI-assisted screening, interview scheduling, and tailored candidate communication.
- Offer universal candidate profile + cross-platform analytics for both seekers and hiring teams.

## Phase 4 — Global Marketplace (18+ Months)
- Scale to multi-region infrastructure with compliance, monitoring, and redundancy.
- Deploy real-time market intelligence (trending skills, salary benchmarks, hiring velocity).
- Unlock predictive matchmaking: success probabilities, retention forecasts, and ecosystem-wide insights.
